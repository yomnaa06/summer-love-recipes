Ingredients

  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup (65g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup (90g) chocolate chips

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 175¬∞C (350¬∞F) with a warm heart.
  2. Gently mix melted butter and sugar, then add eggs and vanilla with love.
  3. Fold in flour, salt, baking powder, and chocolate chips for the brownie base.
  4. Spread in a pan, layer with cookie dough, and bake for 30-35 minutes.
  5. Cool with tenderness and cut into sweet, loving squares.

Pancakes ‚úø

Fluffy pancakes we made to start our summer mornings with a smile! ü•ûüå∏

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (125g) all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 cup (240ml) milk
  • 1 large egg

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt with a gentle touch.
  2. Whisk milk and egg, then blend into dry ingredients with care.
  3. Heat a skillet with love and pour batter into small, happy circles.
  4. Cook until bubbly, flip, and golden, about 2-3 minutes per side.
  5. Serve warm with syrup or fresh berries as a sweet hug.

Tiramisu ‚úø

A creamy Italian treat we crafted with love this summer! üçÆ‚ù•

Ingredients

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup (150g) sugar
  • 1 cup (240ml) mascarpone cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups (360ml) strong coffee, cooled
  • 2 tbsp coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 24 ladyfingers

Instructions

  1. Whisk egg yolks and sugar over a double boiler until thick with care.
  2. Fold in mascarpone cheese until smooth and loving.
  3. Dip ladyfingers in coffee mixture and layer in a dish with tenderness.
  4. Spread mascarpone mixture, repeat layers, and chill for 4 hours.
  5. Dust with cocoa powder for a sweet, tender finish.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (240ml) water
  • 1/2 cup (115g) butter
  • 1 cup (125g) all-purpose flour
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Vegetable oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Boil water, butter, and salt, then add flour to form a dough with care.
  2. Beat in eggs one at a time until smooth and loving.
  3. Pipe into heart shapes and fry in hot oil until golden with a smile.
  4. Drain and roll in cinnamon sugar with tenderness.
  5. Serve warm with a chocolate dip hug.

Cinnamon Rolls ‚úø

Soft rolls of warmth we baked to fill our summer with love! üçû‚ù•

Ingredients

  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 cup (240ml) warm milk
  • 1/4 cup (50g) sugar
  • 3 cups (375g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup (60g) butter, melted
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon

Instructions

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm milk with a pinch of sugar, let sit with love.
  2. Mix flour, sugar, and melted butter, then knead into a tender dough.
  3. Roll out, spread with cinnamon sugar, and roll into a log with care.
  4. Cut into rolls, let rise for 1 hour, and bake at 180¬∞C (350¬∞F) for 20-25 minutes.
  5. Glaze with a sweet hug and serve warm with affection.
